Development Process

Phase 1: User Research

I found that most of our peers of this age are troubled by how to establish close relationships with family members. especially in new environments, target users generally experience social alienation and rely on superficial interactions to maintain social relationships. Especially when facing international students, they often feel lonely, but in Vancouver, when they are lonely, they may choose to play games with friends at home or go to the club to spend time, at this time they will meet many different kinds of people.

Game Interface

The scene design combines the real city with the abstract emotional world. Softness and Contrast: Soft, warm tones symbolize superficial harmony and friendliness. In contrast, cool tones or high-contrast shadows represent hidden emotional levels and the alienation of pseudo-intimacy.
