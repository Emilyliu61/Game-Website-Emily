About Pseudo-intimacy

Players interested in emotions and social interaction，Pseudo-relationships can be described as interactions that give off the appearance of a committed relationship without the depth, intimacy, and commitment that true relationships possess. They are often fueled by convenience, fear of loneliness, or a desire for validation rather than genuine love and connection.Aug 15, 2023-Google

Game Interface-Andy‘s Journey

At the beginning, Andy will write a diary (negative) about the unfairness or loneliness he encountered in this city. He found that everyone was wearing a hypocritical mask. As the game progresses, after the game is successfully completed, Andy will write a new diary about how he took off the NPC's mask step by step through dialogue and felt the beauty of the world.
